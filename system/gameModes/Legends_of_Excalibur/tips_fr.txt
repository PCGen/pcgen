<strong>Bienvenue dans PCGen !</strong><br>Si vous avez besoin d’aide, appuyez sur <em>F1</em> ou bien lisez la documentation accessible par le menu <b>Aide</b>. Le pas-à-pas de création de personnage est bien pour débuter.
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>The "Things to be Done" list in the bottom left of the Summary tab will guide you on what needs to be done for your character. You can even click on an item to be taken to the appropriate tab and field. 
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>PCGen supports a huge range of source books. Some of the most popular combinations have been set up for you in the Basic list of sources. However you can always create your own combinations on the Advanced sources tab and click Save Source Selection so they show up on the Basic list for you next time. 
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>Use the File > Export or File > Print to output great character sheets. We support HTML and PDF output as well as output to files which can be loaded into real time tools.
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>Using the Qualified toggle buttons on each tab will trim the list down to just the options that you can take.
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>The Affordable toggle button on the Purchase tab only shows those items you have the funds for.

# Game mode specific items go here
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>Don't forget, in 3e and 35e mode, bonus skill points from intelligence are only applied at the time, not retroactively, so if you increase your intelligence later on, you will not get bonus skill points from your previous levels.
En cas de modificateur d’Intelligence positif, les langues supplémentaires doivent être choisies au premier niveau, il n’est pas permis de les sélectionner après le premier niveau à moins d’activer la règle maison contraire. Les règles maison se trouvent dans <b>Outils ▸ Préférences</b>.

Au dessus des listes, il y a un champs de recherche ; il suffit d’y taper du texte pour commencer la recherche, le bouton X efface la recherche.
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>You can right-click the heading of the first column in most lists to choose how you want the contents grouped.
Essayez de cliquer du bouton droit dans les listes pour voir des options supplémentaires, y compris achat et vente en gros d’équipement.
Les armes avec amélioration peuvent être crées dans PCGen en sélectionnant une arme de base et en cliquant sur <em>Ajouter personnalisation</em>. Ce qui permet d’ajouter de créer des armes de maître, des bonus magiques, des matériaux spécifiques, etc. pour créer le parfait donneur de mort.
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>On the Spells > Prepared Spells Tab you'll see Spell List field.  You can add new spell lists (sets of spells you have memorized e.g City, Dungeoneering, Wilderness travel) by adding a new name in the text box and clicking on the Add button.
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>On the Spell Tab to see the number of spells you can cast in a day you need to select your spell casting class in the left-hand pane.
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>The Inventory > Purchase tab shows all of the gear you own. You need to equip the gear on the Inventory > Equipping tab before it affects your character though. You can even set up groups of equipment that you'd use (e.g. City, Party, Combat).  You can then print these sets out showing the changed effects on your movement and skills!
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>If you ever see something that doesn't make sense to you, look on the trouble-shooting preview. To get to that, click on the "Character Sheet" tab. Select the "Troubleshooting.htm" sheet. You will see all the various abilities, skills, hit points, saving throws, and the like all expanded with a tag in [] showing where it came from. Very useful for tracking down that strange -1 that shows up.
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>You can save buffs with your character - just apply them on the Temporary Bonuses tab. The effects of the buffs will then be included when you output your character.  
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>The -2 circumstance penalty for not having thieves tools is a temporary bonus you can apply in the Temporary Bonus Tab. Other circumstantial bonuses such as riding without a saddle or having your familiar within 5' are available in this tab as well.
Il y a de nombreux moyens de personnaliser PCGen. Utilisez <b>Outils ▸ Préférences</b> pour y parvenir.
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>PCGEN doesn't always deal with leveling down perfectly.  So before you level up a character, make sure that you back up the pcg file first. That way if you choose the wrong class etc., you can go back and do it properly.
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>Confused about gold in your inventory?  Remember to purchase coins, gems etc. with your pooled gold to get them shown on your inventory. Ideally the pooled gold should always be at 0.
<em>Not yet translated</em><br>You will see small &gt;, &lt;, &lt;up&gt; and &lt;down&gt; arrows on the panes of the PCGEN windows. You can click on these to get one of the panes to expand to its full width/length.
