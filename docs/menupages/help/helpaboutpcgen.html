About PCGen

The About option displays info about the release version/date of PCGen, as well as listing the main contributors. There are far too many contributors to list them all here since PCGen is very much a community effort. Info on where to get new releases of PCGen and how to find the PCGen community is also provided. Clicking on either of the WWW site or the Email List links will take you to the appropriate web sites that are listed within the button.

The Code, List, Test and Engineering tabs will display a list of the contributors by their general Monkey Groupings.
